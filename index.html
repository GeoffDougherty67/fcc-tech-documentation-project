How to Make Your Own Cocktail

What kind of cocktail?
Decide what you what your cocktail will taste and look like.
  1. Who is it for?
  2. When is it for?
  3. How rare are the ingredients?
  4. How does it look?
  5. How is it prepared?

All these factors are important to consider when designing a cocktail. The order that they should be considered will depend on the specific circumstances of each creation.

For instance if the cocktail is designed specifically to feature in a contest, rare ingredients may be perferred. However if the cocktail is being created for a dive bare with a dancefloor, you will want to work with ingredients already available or very easily obtained.
